About Morioh

Morioh (杜王町 Moriō-chō) is a town located within Japan's M Prefecture in the city of S. It has a small population and is very peaceful.

This Town is full of marvelous attractions, landmarks and rich Japanese history. It's a primarily suburban city, near the coast, filled with a lively, helpful and kind community of hardworking people.
